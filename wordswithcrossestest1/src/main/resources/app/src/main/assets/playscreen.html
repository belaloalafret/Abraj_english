Play Board Help

On this screen you solve your puzzle!

Zoom and Sizes:

  • For Zoom controls or to embiggen the clue text, long touch the play board.

Pressing the Menu button provides the following options:

  • Show Errors
    Highlights unsolved or incorrect boxes in red. (May be disabled if no answer key is available)
  • Reveal
    Presents the option to reveal the correct solution to a box, word or the whole puzzle. Once revealed "cheated" boxes have a pink background. (May be disabled if no answer key is available)
  • Clues
    Takes you to a screen where you can browse the across and down clues for the whole puzzle. You can also reach this screen by tapping the clue line at the top of the screen.
  • Info
    Shows information about the puzzle you are playing.
  • Help
    Displays this screen
  • Settings
    Takes you to the application settings screen.

There is not yet any way to enter multiple letters into a single square (aka a rebus puzzle). If you encounter a rebus, just enter the first letter of the rebus, and it will be counted as correct.
